OIZYS

Oizys explores the journey towards acceptance of two siblings who have embarked on a new experimental mind-altering simulation designed to 'aid' in complicated grief. After the death of their father, two siblings are encouraged by their mother to join an experimental program targeted towards individuals who are struggling on their grief journey. In hopes of moving on and out of their depressive states and even resentful nature towards each other, the pair reluctantly agree. Through various scenarios, each dedicated to the respective stages of grief, the two dive into themselves and into understanding the other as they venture through a "world" that becomes more and more emotionally disturbing. Will they come to terms with their reality, or will they be stuck in a timeless, never-ending loop of denial?
